Privacy Policy for CoverMix

Last updated: 12/09/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to CoverMix. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect

When you use CoverMix, we collect and process the following data:

  • Your Spotify account email address (only when you connect your Spotify account)
  • Playlist data, including track names and audio features (when you submit a playlist for processing)
  • Usage data (how you interact with our app)

We do not collect or store your Spotify password or any payment information.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Generate custom cover art for your playlists
  • Provide playlist analytics
  • Improve our services
  • Communicate with you about our service

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Spotify (to access your playlist information)
  • Our service providers (e.g., hosting providers)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Request transfer of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
